Refrigerant Recovery Forms

To comply with refrigerant recovery requirements:

Manufacturers must fill out: Company Profile Form.

Stewardship organizations must fill out: Stewardship Organization Profile Form.

Please note that if you are a manufacturer submitting as part of a stewardship organization, you’ll need to submit a Company Profile, not a Stewardship Organization Profile Form.

An Annual Report is due on July 1 from any manufacturer who elects to operate their own refrigerant recovery program, or participate in a refrigerant recovery program with other manufacturers.
