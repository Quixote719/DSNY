Putrescible Transfer Stations Quarterly Report

Local Law 40 of 1990 requires that each operator of a solid waste transfer station complete a Private Transfer Station Solid Waste/Recycling Quarterly Report.

The report provides information relating to the quantity and type of material received at your facility, as well as the landfills and recycling vendors where materials are delivered.
