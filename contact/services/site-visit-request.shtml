Site Visit Request

If you receive DSNY collection service, you can request a site visit for DSNY to review recycling regulations with building management and custodial staff during normal business hours.

If you’re not sure if your location receives DSNY collection, please check with your facilities manager or custodian. Depending on availability, DSNY may also give recycling presentations to tenant organizations or community groups.
