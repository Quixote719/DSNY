Request Professional Fee Collection

DSNY is authorized to provide fee-based collection and disposal service for designated recyclables and regular garbage generated by professional services doing business within residential buildings.

You may quality if you are located in a Sanitation serviced building and meet any one of the following:

 

  • Authorized to engage in an occupation in a residential location that also serves as your home.
  • You or your group are licensed New York State physicians, dentists, accountants, lawyers, chiropractors, or other licensed professionals whose practice is located in a basement or on the first or second floor of a residential building.
  • You are engaged in a profession located in a residential portion of the building that has been used for the same occupational purpose since December 1961.

 

If you are interested in applying for this service, print out the application and follow the instructions.
