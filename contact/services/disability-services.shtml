Disability Services

The New York City Department of Sanitation complies with all applicable laws, including, but not limited to, the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Rehabilitation Act.

Anyone who requires services or a modification of policies or procedures to participate in DSNY programs or activities is encouraged to contact the Disability Services Facilitator by telephone or email:
