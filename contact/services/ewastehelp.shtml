Other TV peripherals
TV peripherals, other than those listed above, are auxiliaries that connects to and works with the TV in some way. Such as: Chromecast, Speakers, Streaming boxes.
Other portable devices
Portable devices, other than those listed above, are any devices that can easily be carried. Such as: Phones, MP3, watches.
Other computer peripherals
Computer peripherals, other than those listed above, are auxiliaries that connects to and works with the computer in some way. Such as: microphones, loudspeakers, webcams, and digital cameras.
