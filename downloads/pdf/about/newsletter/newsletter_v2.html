Doing Good
Austism Awareness
For Autism Awareness Month each April, our personnel support Autism Speaks. Since 2014, we raised more than $100,000 for Autism Speaks. The Department also helped raise New Yorkers’ awareness about autism spectrum disorder by displaying Autism Speaks “Light It Up Blue” posters on some 2,500 collection trucks and mechanical brooms.
