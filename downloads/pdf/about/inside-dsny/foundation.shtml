Foundation for New York's Strongest

The Foundation for New York’s Strongest is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the work of the New York City Department of Sanitation. The Foundation, established in 2016, aims to educate New Yorkers in protecting our environment, highlight DSNY workers’ service and commitment in keeping New York City healthy, safe and clean, and one day, create a new, educational DSNY museum.

"Uniform" Heron Preston for DSNY Fashion Event

Heron’s Vision: Create a ready-to-wear collection repurposing DSNY uniforms – a fashion line that keeps used clothing from decomposing in landfill – while celebrating the emergency responders who are often invisible to the 8.5 million people they serve.

The Result: UNIFORM, a unique fashion line comprised of one-of-a-kind pieces, to be presented at the critically acclaimed and closed-to-the-public Spring Street salt shed – a venue that the New York Times called “New York’s best new public sculpture.”

Known for his collaborations with Kanye West and design work at Nike, Heron’s couture will draw tremendous media coverage – from New York City news outlets to significant fashion publications.

To purchase tickets to the event that support the Foundation for New York’s Strongest, Inc, visit the event website. The funds raised will help DSNY lead New Yorkers to send zero waste to landfills by 2030 – an initiative known as 0x30, or zero-by-thirty.
