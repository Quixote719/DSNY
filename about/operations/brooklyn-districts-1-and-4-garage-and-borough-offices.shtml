DSNY Brooklyn Districts 1 and 4 Garage and Borough Offices

The Brooklyn Districts 1 and 4 Garage and Borough Offices are a DSNY vehicle maintenance center of architectural esteem.

The garage accommodates 134 large trucks, 18 small trucks, 28 small fleet vehicles, and a substantial salt storage shed. The site is designed to support the constant flow of large sanitation vehicles through its doors, offering access to fully operating repair bays throughout.

Paying homage to civic duty, the garage seamlessly connects to the Brooklyn Borough Administrative Office’s garage via Schole Street. Their union emphasizes the garage’s dedication to servicing the borough of Brooklyn.
