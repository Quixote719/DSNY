DSNY Info

DSNY Info is the NYC Department of Sanitation’s official app. It’s free to download and gives New Yorkers:

  • Collection reminders;
  • Alerts when holidays or severe weather will affect collections – and when the service will resume;
  • Tips on how to safely dispose of potentially hazardous products, such as medication, paint or chemicals;
  • Easy ways to give new life to used things through donateNYC; and
  • Information on events, including Pumkin Smash, SAFE Disposal, Mulchfest and compost givebacks.
