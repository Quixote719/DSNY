Snow Alert Issued

Tuesday, January 25, 2011 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 11-06, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation has issued a snow alert for Wednesday, January 26, 2011 starting at 7 AM.

A snow alert is a readiness notification of a possible snow storm alerting DSNY personnel and Starting initial equipment readiness for storm response. In a snow alert, salt spreaders are loaded and plows.
