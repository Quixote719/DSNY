Snow Alert Issued

Monday, January 31, 2011 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 11-09, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation has issued a snow alert for Tuesday, February 1, 2011 starting at 1 A.M.

A snow alert is a readiness notification of a possible snow storm alerting DSNY personnel and starting initial equipment readiness for storm response. In a snow alert, salt spreaders are loaded and plows attached.
