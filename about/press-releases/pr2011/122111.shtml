Funeral Arrangements Set for Sanitation Worker Thomas Lermand

December 21,2011 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 11-54, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Funeral arrangements have been finalized for Sanitation Worker Thomas Lermand, 48, who collapsed and died while on duty Saturday, December 17th, in Brooklyn.

Assigned to the Department’s Brooklyn South District 18, which covers Canarsie and Marine Park, Sanitation Worker Lermand, a 16-year veteran, leaves behind his wife, Barbara, and two daughters.

Viewing will be Thursday, December 22nd, from 6:00 PM to 9:00 P.M.  A full inspector’s funeral will be held Friday, December 23rd at 10:00 A.M. at the Guarino Funeral Home, 9222 Flatlands Avenue, Brooklyn, NY.
