Snow Alert Issued

Monday, January 24, 2011 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 11-05, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation has issued a snow alert for Tuesday, January 25, 2011 starting at 12:01 A.M. for the potential of a light dusting of snow only.

A snow alert is a readiness notification of a possible snow event wherein DSNY personnel begin initial equipment readiness, such as loading salt spreaders and attaching plows, as needed.
