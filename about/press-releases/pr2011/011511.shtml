Snow Alert Issued

Friday, January 14, 2011 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 11-03, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation has issued a snow alert for Saturday, January 15, 2011 starting at 4:00 P.M. for a light dusting only.

A snow alert is a readiness notification of a possible snow event wherein DSNY personnel begin initial equipment readiness, such as loading salt spreaders and attaching plows, as needed.
