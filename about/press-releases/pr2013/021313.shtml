No Refuse, Recycling Collection or Street Cleaning on President's Day, Monday 2/18/13

February 11,2013 | Vito A. Turso

Press Release # 13-08, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation (DSNY) announced today that in observance of Presidents’ Day, there will be no refuse or recycling, or street cleaning on Monday, February 18, 2013.  In addition, all Sanitation Department administrative offices will be closed on Monday, February 18th, in observance of the holiday.

Residents who normally receive Monday refuse collection should place their trash out at the curbside Monday after 4 PM for pickup. Those receiving Monday recycling collection will not have their recycling picked up until the following Monday, February 25th.

For additional information about Sanitation services and holiday schedules, contact 3-1-1 or visit the DSNY website at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
