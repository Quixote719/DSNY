Regular Scheduled Garbage and Recycling Collection on Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, Monday, January 21, 2002

January 7, 2002 | Kathy Dawkins / Al Ferguson (646) 885-5020

Press Release # 02-03, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation has announced that, on Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, there will be garbage and recycling collection on Monday, January 21, 2002.

However, there will be no street cleaning on Monday, January 21, 2002.

The Department’s administrative offices will be closed on Monday, January 21, 2002 in observance of the holiday. For questions regarding Sanitation services and schedules, contact the Sanitation Action Center at (212) 219-8090 or visit our web site at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
