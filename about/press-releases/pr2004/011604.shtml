Regularly Scheduled Garbage and Recycling Collection on Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, Monday, January 19, 2004

January 16, 2004 | Vito A. Turso / Keith W. Mellis (646) 885-5020

Press Release # 04-02, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

However, Alternate Side Parking Regulations will be Suspended on Monday, January 19, 2004

The New York City Department of Sanitation will conduct normal refuse and recycling collection on Monday, January 19, 2004, Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day.

In addition, the Department's administrative offices will be closed in observance of the holiday on Monday, January 19, 2004.

For questions regarding Sanitation services and schedules, contact the Citizens Service Center 311 or visit the DSNY web site at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
