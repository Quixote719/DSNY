Regularly scheduled collection on Monday, Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Day

Tuesday, January 9, 2007 | Vito A. Turso / Matthew Li Pani (646) 885-5020

Press Release # 07-01, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

But Alternate Side Parking regulations will be suspended

The New York City Department of Sanitation will conduct normal refuse and recycling collection on Monday, January 15, 2007, the observance of the Rev. Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day. Residents who receive Monday refuse and recycling collection should place their items out as usual at curbside Sunday after 4 P.M. for pickup the following day. However, Alternate Side Parking regulations will be suspended on Monday, January 15th in observance of the holiday.

In addition, the Department's administrative offices will be closed in observance of the holiday on Monday, January 15th.

For questions regarding Sanitation services and schedules, contact the Citizens Service Center at 3-1-1 or visit the DSNY website at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
