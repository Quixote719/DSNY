Regularly Scheduled Garbage and Recycling Collection on Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, Monday, January 20, 2003

January 9, 2003 | Contact: John Pampalone (646) 885-5020

Press Release # 03-02, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation will conduct normal refuse and recycling collection on Monday, January 20, 2003, Martin Luther King, Jr. Day.

However, there will be no street cleaning on Monday, January 20, 2003.

In addition, the Department's administrative offices will be closed in observance of the holiday on Monday, January 20, 2003.

For questions regarding Sanitation services and schedules, contact Sanitation at (212) 219-8090 or visit the DSNY web site at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
