Funeral Services set for Sanitation Work Killed on Brooklyn-Queens Expressway

August 29, 2006 | Vito A. Turso / Matthew LiPani(646) 885-5020

Press Release # 06-38, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

 

Funeral arrangements have been finalized for Sanitation Worker Michael Occhino, 25, killed in the line of duty on Monday, August 28th in a tragic accident on the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway.

Assigned to Sanitation’s Brooklyn South District 11 in Bensonhurst, Sanitation Worker Occhino leaves behind his parents, a grandfather, and his twin brother Carmine, a NYC firefighter.

 

The viewing is being held at 
Scarpaci Funeral Home 
1401 86th Street Brooklyn, New York 
August 29th and Wednesday, August 30th 
from 2 to 5 PM and 7 to 9:30 PM.

A full Inspector’s Funeral will be held on:  
St. Bernadette Roman Catholic Church 
8201 13th Avenue 
Brooklyn, New York
Thursday, August 31, 2006 @ 10:30 AM  

Burial will follow at: 
Greenwood Cemetery 
25th Street & 5th Avenue 
Brooklyn, New York
