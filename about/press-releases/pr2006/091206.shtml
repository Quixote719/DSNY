Press Release # 06-43, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

 

Funeral arrangements have been finalized for Sanitation Worker Allen Gormely, 53, who died Monday of an apparent heart attack while on collection duty. 

Assigned to Sanitation's Brooklyn South District 13, which covers Brighton Beach, Sanitation Worker Gormely, a 21 year veteran of the DSNY, leaves behind his wife Joanne, two daughters, Christina and Melissa, a son, Justin, and son-in-law, Anthony Scotto, both of whom are Sanitation Workers, and three grandchildren.

Viewing will be held at the Peter C. LaBella Funeral Home, 2625 Harway Avenue, Brooklyn, on Wednesday, September 13th and Thursday, September 14th from 2 PM to 5 PM, and 7 PM to 9:30 PM.  A full inspector's funeral will be held on:

Friday, September 15, 2006 at 10:30 AM
at
Our Lady of Solace Church
1717 Mermaid Avenue
Brooklyn
