Regularly Scheduled Collection on Martin Luther King Jr. Day 01/16/06

January 11, 2006 | Vito A. Turso / Matthew LiPani (646) 885-5020

Press Release # 06-01, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

However, Alternate Side Parking Regulations will be suspended on Monday, January 16th.

The New York City Department of Sanitation will conduct normal refuse and recycling collection on Monday, January 16, 2006, the observance of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day. Residents who receive Monday garbage and recycling collection should place their items out at curbside Sunday after 5 P.M. for pickup the following day as normal.

However, the Department's administrative offices will be closed in observance of the holiday on Monday, January 16th. For questions regarding Sanitation services and schedules, contact the Citizens Service Center at 311 or visit the DSNY website at www.nyc.gov/sanitation.
