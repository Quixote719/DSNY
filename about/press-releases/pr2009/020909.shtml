No Garbage, Recycling Collection or Street Cleaning on President's Day Monday, February 16th

February 9,2009 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 09-07, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The New York City Department of Sanitation (DSNY) announced today that in observance of Presidents' Day, there will be no garbage or recycling collection, or street cleaning on Monday, February 16, 2009.  In addition, all Sanitation Department administrative offices will be closed on Monday, February 16th, in observance of the holiday.

Residents who normally receive Monday garbage collection should place their trash out at curbside Monday after 4 P.M. for pickup.  Those receiving Monday recycling collection will not have their recycling picked up until the following Monday, February 23rd.  

For questions about Sanitation services and holiday schedules, contact 311 or visit the DSNY website at www.nyc.gov/sanitation .
