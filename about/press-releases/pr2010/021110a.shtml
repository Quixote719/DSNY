Limited Refuse and Recycling Collections Resume Tomorrow

Thursday, February 11, 2010 | Vito A. Turso/Matthew LiPani

Press Release # 10-08, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

The Department of Sanitation will resume limited refuse and recycling collections throughout the city tomorrow.

Residents whose refuse and recycling was not collected earlier this week because of the snowstorm - - and those with Friday refuse pickups - - should place their material out at curbside after 4 P.M. this evening. Residents are asked to place their material out in a cleared area, if possible, to help Sanitation Workers gain access to the cans and bags.

However, those with Friday recycling collection - - which would not have been collected Friday, February 12th because of the Lincoln's Day holiday - - will have their recycling picked up on Friday, February 19th.
