Compost Project Materials

The NYC Department of Sanitation has created materials to assist and encourage NYC residents, agencies, and institutions to compost food and yard waste.

The links below open pdfs of the current materials available for download, or order these free materials online: Recycling & Waste Prevention Material Request.

NOTE: These links open PDF (Portable Document Format) documents; you'll need the free Adobe Acrobat Reader to display and print them.

Composting

  • Compost Project Brochure: Explains the various programs of the NYC Compost Project.
  • Outdoor Composting Guide: Describes how to compost food & yard waste on-site, using outdoor composting bins.
  • Indoor Worm Bin Composting Brochure: Describes how to compost food & yard waste on-site, using indoor worm composting bins.
  • NYC Master Composter Manual: Instruction manual that accompanies the Master Composter Certificate Program.
