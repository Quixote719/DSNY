Composting Opportunities, 2012

In 2010, New York City Council passed Local Law 42, which requires the New York City Department of Sanitation and the Mayor’s Office of Long-term Planning and Sustainability to issue a report recommending methods to expand the diversion of compostable waste from the city's waste stream.

Local Law 42: A 2012 Assessment of Composting Opportunities in NYC explores organics processing capacity in the New York City region, and the existing and potential options to divert compostable waste from households and institutions.
