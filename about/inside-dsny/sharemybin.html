Share My Bin

What is this about?

Some of your neighbors aren't in the NYC Organics Collection Pilot Program and they'd like to keep their food scraps, food-soiled paper and yard trimmings from being treated like garbage.

But what do I have to do?

Almost nothing! Just take the sticker below and add it to your bin. That way, when it's at the curb, your neighbors will know they can share your bin!

So, what is the big deal?

We are turning your organic waste into soil-enriching compost for our street plants, community gardens and public parks. Soon we'll use it to create clean, renewable energy to heat homes and businesses across our City!
