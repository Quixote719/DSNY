NYC's Recycling Curriculum

DSNY developed the K-12 School RRResource Guide: RRR You Ready? to help educators implement the three R's (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) in NYC schools.

The RRR Guide includes:

  • Lesson plans and activity sheets for grades K-12 that comply with Department of Education standards.
  • Ideas for hands-on projects and long-term activities.
  • Extensive background information, including glossary sheets.
