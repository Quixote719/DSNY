Job Training program

If you are a business and interested in hiring employees, DSNY’s Job Training Program (JTP) can help.

DSNY Job Training Program participants complete six months of comprehensive on-the-job skills training in custodial, clerical, and soft skills such as how to keep a job. JTP participants also receive job-readiness skills development on how to update their resumes, conduct mock interviews, and apply online for both City and private jobs.

DSNY graduates have the training and experience to qualify for a variety of employment opportunities including:

  • Clerical
  • Maintenance
  • Street cleaning

Employers save time and money hiring candidates who are pre-screened to meet job requirements.

At this time, DSNY does not accept outside referrals for the Job Training Program. For general inquiries or to discuss your hiring needs, please complete the Job Training Inquiry.
