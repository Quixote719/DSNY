School Recycling Materials

The NYC Department of Sanitation has created materials to assist and encourage NYC residents, agencies, and institutions to recycle, compost, and prevent waste.

The links below open pdfs of the current materials available for download, or order these free materials online Recycling & Waste Prevention Material Request

NOTE: These links open PDF (Portable Document Format) documents; you'll need the free Adobe Acrobat Reader to display and print them.

Schools – Educational

(These materials are for schools ONLY)

  • NYC K-12 School RRResource Guide: (320 pages in 1" binder) Includes reduce, reuse & recycling activities and lesson plans that meet current education standards.
  • DSNY Coloring Book: For Pre-K to Grade 1 (pre-literate).
  • DSNY Comic Book, Grades 2-3: For Grades 2 to 3 (emergent readers).
  • DSNY Comic Book, Grades 4-6: For Grades 4 to 6 (fluent readers).

Recycling Info

  • NYC Schools Guide to Zero Waste: (28 pages) Comprehensive guide to proper recycling and organics setup, and other resources for schools. Intended for school custodians, administrators, teachers, and Green Teams.
  • NYC Schools Guide to Zero Waste: accessible PDF
  • How To Recycle removable sticker: Shows what to recycle. Appropriate to distribute to all staff, students, and parents.
  • How To Recycle flyer (English/Spanish): Shows what to recycle. English/Spanish. Appropriate to distribute to all staff, students, and parents.
  • How To Recycle flyer (English/Chinese): Shows what to recycle. English/Chinese. Appropriate to distribute to all staff, students, and parents.
  • Bookmark - Zero Waste: What to recycle info and website links. Appropriate to distribute to all staff, students, and parents.

  • Green Indoor Decal: To label indoor recycling containers for mixed paper & cardboard.
  • Green Outdoor Decal: To label outdoor paper recycling containers or dumpsters for mixed paper & cardboard.
  • "Mixed Paper" Text Decal: (14" x 1.25") To label indoor recycling container lids.
  • "Trash Only" Text Decal: (14" x 3") To label lids or sides of indoor trash containers.
