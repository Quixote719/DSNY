Businesses in NYC: Avoiding Common Sanitation Violations

The NYC Department of Sanitation keeps our City healthy, safe and clean by collecting, recycling and disposing waste, cleaning streets and vacant lots, and clearing snow and ice. Businesses, however, must hire private carting companies for collection and disposal service. Sanitation’s Enforcement Division monitors compliance with the laws governing clean streets – and business owners can avoid the most common fines by following these guidelines.
