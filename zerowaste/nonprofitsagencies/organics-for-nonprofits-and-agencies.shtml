Organics for Nonprofits & Agencies

Compost yard waste or food scraps to reduce waste and help build healthy soil.

Learn about on-site composting.

NYC Organics Collection is available to nonprofit organizations and agencies in select areas. Organic waste includes yard waste, food scraps, and compostable paper (such as napkins and paper plates).

To request NYC Organics Collection, complete the online inquiry form
