Donate + Get Donations For Schools

NYC Department of Education schools must follow relinquishment guidelines for discarding obsolete equipment and furniture. To donate usable equipment, visit donateNYC.

Organizations and reuse programs provide schools with new and used goods, including supplies, books and computers. You or your company can reduce waste and help our schools by donating useful items. Schools can utilize these programs to get donations.
