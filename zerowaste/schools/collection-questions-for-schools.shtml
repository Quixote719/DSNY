Collection Questions For Schools

If you think DSNY missed a collection of garbage or recyclables from your school, contact your local DSNY garage by calling 311.

If you have a service issue, require a special pickup for a large cleanout, or have other questions, refer to the Communications Protocol:

  • DOE DSNY Waste Protocol (PDF)
  • DSNY Private schools Communication Protocol (PDF)

DSNY accepts complaints about missed collections starting at 8 am on the day after the scheduled collection.
