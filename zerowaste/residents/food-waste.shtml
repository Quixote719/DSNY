Food Scraps

Garbage: Discard on regular pickup days.

Organics Collection: Many NYC residents keep their food scraps, food-soiled paper, and yard waste separated from other waste. Food scraps are collected separately from buildings that receive curbside organics collection.

You can also compost at home or at neighborhood food scrap drop-off sites.

Recycling: Empty and rinse metal, glass, plastic, or carton food containers and place them in your blue-labeled bin for recycling.

Donations & Reuse
