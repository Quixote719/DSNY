What Can't Go in Recycling & Garbage for Residents

These items can’t be put out with recyclables or regular garbage, or may require special handling:

  • Acids, such as hydrofluoric, sulfuric, and muriatic acids
  • Asbestos
  • Certain Batteries, either auto batteries or rechargeable batteries
  • Commercial waste
  • Certain electronic equipment
  • Gasoline
  • Highly corrosive or flammable liquids, such as acid or gasoline
  • Mercury containing devices—except for fluorescent bulbs
  • Motor oil
  • Propane tanks and gas cylinders
  • Tires
  • Wood debris
  • Yard waste from professional landscapers
