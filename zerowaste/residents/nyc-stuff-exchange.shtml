NYC Stuff Exchange

The NYC Stuff Exchange, developed by DSNY, offers a quick and simple online tool to find out where to donate, buy, or sell gently used goods in NYC.

  • Hundreds of listings, searchable by zip code, borough, or citywide.
  • Nonprofits promote upcoming donation drives, swaps, book drives, and rummage sales through the Calendar of Events.
  • Mixed Establishments:Non-food businesses (offices, stores, supermarkets) with a kitchen, cafeteria or dining/drinking area
  • Get listed explains how to request a free listing or submit an event to the calendar.
