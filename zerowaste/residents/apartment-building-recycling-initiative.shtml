Apartment Building Recycling Initiative

The Department will be offering a new recycling training program for building supers and property managers in 2016. It will cover recycling and set-out requirements, plus how to participate in DSNY's zero-waste programs, such as refashionNYC, ecycleNYC and organics collection. Until then:

  • Property managers and supers seeking help to improve recycling in an apartment building, please request a site visit;
  • If you would like a recycling training for your residents, contact GrowNYC about their services and programs;
  • Use DSNY’s online order form to order free recycling decals and signs for a residential building; and
  • Submit an Apartment Programs Inquiry to sign up for refashioNYC, ecycleNYC, or organics collection in your building.
