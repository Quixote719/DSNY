Furniture

Recycling: If the furniture is predominantly metal or rigid plastic, set it out next to your recycling container on your recycling collection day.

Garbage: If the furniture is predominantly wood or fabric, place it out with trash. Wrap mattresses in plastic and place on the curb with regular trash.
