Medical Equipment

DSNY Collection

Recycling: Medical equipment that is mostly metal or rigid plastic can be placed at the curb for recycling with metal, glass, and plastic.

Garbage: Discard other items with regular trash.

Donations & Reuse

These organizations collect usable medical equipment and supplies and medicines, for distribution in areas with medical emergencies or in developing nations. Check their websites for information about the equipment and supplies they accept and for drop-off locations:

  • Afya Foundation
  • Aid for Aids
  • Global Crisis Interventions
  • Meds for MADRE
  • Assistive Technology Exchange in New England & New York
