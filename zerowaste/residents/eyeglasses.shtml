Eyeglasses

Garbage: Discard with regular trash on pickup day.

Donations & Reuse

Donate or sell used eyeglasses that are in working condition. These organizations accept donations; check their websites for details:
  • Lions Club/Recycle For Sight
  • New Eyes for the Needy
You can also donate prescription eyeglasses at collection boxes located at LensCrafters, Goodwill stores, or members of the American Optometric Association.
