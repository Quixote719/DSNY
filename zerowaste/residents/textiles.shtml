Textiles

Garbage: You can throw out textile items in your regular garbage.

Donations & Reuse

Sign up for re-fashioNYC, a partnership between NYC and Housing Works that provides convenient, in-building drop-off service for clothing, accessories, and textiles. Free donation bins for qualifying apartment buildings (with 10 or more units) and businesses that regularly collect and donate clothing. Use the online enrollment program inquiry form.

Visit NYC Stuff Exchange to find a place to sell or donate gently used clothing and textiles, or bring them to a community swap.

Here are additional options for donating particular items.

Professional clothing:

  • Bottomless Closet
  • Career Gear
  • Dress for Success

Winter coats:

  • NY Cares

Shoes:

  • Shoe4Africa
  • Soles4Souls

Clothing and linens for bedding and bathing animals (especially towels):

  • The American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA)
  • Animal Care & Control of New York City
  • The Humane Society of New York

Costumes:

  • TDF Costume Collection–clothing and costumes from the 1950s and earlier

Take-Back or Drop-Off

Design Tex can provide ship-to information for recycling or reclamation of many of their upholstery, panel, and drapery fabrics.

The Nike Reuse-A-Shoe Program recycles the rubber, foam, and fabric from any brand of used sneakers into padded flooring.

The Patagonia Common Threads Garment Recycling Program recycles Polartec fleece, Patagonia organic t-shirts, and Capilene Performance Baselayers into new Patagonia clothing.
