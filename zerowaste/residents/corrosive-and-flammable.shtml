Highly Corrosive and Flammable

Products labeled “DANGER-corrosive” and “DANGER-flammable” cannot be disposed of in regular garbage or recycling. Dispose of empty containers with regular garbage, not recycling. Submerge flares in water and saturate thoroughly to render unusable, then bag and place out with your regular garbage.

SAFE Disposal Events & Drop-Off Sites

Corrosive and flammable materials are accepted at SAFE Disposal Events.

Purchasing Tips

Only buy as much as you need and use the entire product if possible.
