Pesticides

Recycling: Dispose of empty containers that are not labeled “DANGER–corrosive” with your regular recycling.

Garbage: You can throw out small quantities of pesticides with your curbside garbage. First dry out any liquids with kitty litter, newspaper, or other absorbent material. For containers that are labeled “DANGER–corrosive,” double-bag the empty or dried out containers and mark the bag “DANGER–not for recycling” before putting in the trash.

SAFE Disposal Events

Pesticides are accepted at SAFE Disposal Events.
