Collection Schedule For Residents

Set out your garbage, organics, and recycling after 4 pm the night before your scheduled collection day. Remove empty containers by 9 pm on the collection day, or by 9 am the following morning if your collection occurs after 4 pm.

To learn your collection days, enter your building number and the name of your street, select borough, then click Search.
(For example, 125 Worth Street is: BUILDING NUMBER: 125, STREET NAME: Worth Street)

To learn if your neighborhood receives organics collection service, see the map of participating neighborhoods (pdf).

During snow operations, recycling and garbage collection may be disrupted while DSNY plows and cleans the streets.

Holidays & Holiday Setout Instructions

For specific post-holiday collection information, read our most recent press releases or follow DSNY on Twitter or Facebook. There are no collections on these holidays:

  • New Year's Day
  • Martin Luther King Day
  • Lincoln's Birthday
  • President's Day
  • Memorial Day
  • Independence Day
  • Labor Day
  • Columbus Day
  • Election Day
  • Veterans' Day
  • Thanksgiving Day
  • Christmas Day

Here's when to set out your garbage and recycling on holidays: (Remember, it’s not unusual for your collection time to change a bit during a holiday week.)

Garbage: Set out after 4 p.m. on the evening of the holiday, for collection beginning the next day.

Recycling: Set out recyclables after 4 p.m. on the evening of the holiday for collection beginning the next day.

Organics (only for buildings with NYC Organics Collection): Set out food scraps, food-soiled paper, and yard waste after 4 p.m. on the evening of the holiday, for collection beginning the next day.

For specific post-holiday collection information, read our most recent press releases or follow DSNY on Twitter or Facebook.
