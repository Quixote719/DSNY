Food Waste Carter Services

In the summer of 2015, DSNY and the Business Integrity Commission (BIC) conducted an informal carter services survey in order to assist businesses in finding a private carter that suites their needs. The survey was sent to all NYC licensed carters, but completing the survey was voluntary. As such, not all private carters responded. The results of this survey were self-reported by the private carter and were not verified for accuracy by DSNY or BIC.
