What to Recycle for Construction Projects

Any business undergoing construction is responsible for ensuring that the construction waste is recycled.

What To Recycle

Construction waste is any material that is generated from a construction project, with the exception of:

  • Plaster
  • Wall coverings
  • Drywall
  • Roofing shingles
  • Wood and lumber
  • Glass window panes

What Goes Where

Keep construction waste recyclables separate from non-recyclable garbage and other recyclables.

Who Picks It Up

For information on choosing a private carter and setting out recycling and garbage, refer to Setout Rules for Businesses.
