What Can't Go in Recycling and Garbage for Businesses

Some commonly used items cannot go in the regular garbage or recycling. These items include hazardous waste like batteries, anything containing mercury, pesticides, medical waste, and certain types of lights.

The following items cannot be thrown out with regular garbage:

Automotive Products

Batteries

Construction & Demolition Waste

Electronics

Harmful Products (Items containing mercury and pesticides)

Fluorescent Lighting

Medical Waste
