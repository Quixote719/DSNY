Trainings

To help businesses avoid violations and improve recycling, DSNY offers free trainings for business owners and employees, building management companies, and chambers/associations. Learn more and reserve a spot in an upcoming training.

Trade groups, chambers of commerce, merchant associations, BIDs, operators of multiple locations, and other business groups willing to host and recruit members can request a training by emailing commercialprograms@dsny.nyc.gov. Be sure to include "Training" in the subject line of the email. There must be representatives from at least 5 unique locations in order to host a training.

Signage

DSNY does not print or distribute signage to private businesses; businesses should not be using residential signage printed by DSNY to comply with rules. However, in order to assist businesses in posting appropriate signage, we have developed basic sample signs. Businesses may also find it helpful to investigate signage available from Recycle Across America.

Businesses required or interested in take-back programs can download signs.
